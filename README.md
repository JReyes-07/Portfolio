# Data_Analytics_Portfolio
My data analytics portfolio projects.

## Storytelling With Data

[Link to project](https://public.tableau.com/views/StorytellingProjectReturnRates/StoryArc?:language=en-US\&:sid=\&:redirect=auth\&:display\_count=n\&:origin=viz\_share\_link)

This project analyzes the factors contributing to the Superstore’s high rate of returned orders. The goal is to provide the CEO with data-driven insights into why customers are returning products and to recommend strategies for reducing return rates and improving overall business performance.

## Data Visualization w/ Tableau

[Link to project](https://public.tableau.com/views/DataVisualizationProject\_17731265523370/ProfitsLossesQ1?:language=en-US\&:sid=\&:redirect=auth\&:display\_count=n\&:origin=viz\_share\_link)

This project evaluates the Superstore’s operations to identify opportunities for improving profitability and avoiding financial decline. The analysis focuses on identifying the largest sources of profit and loss, recommending products and subcategories to retain or discontinue, determining the most effective state and seasonal advertising opportunities based on return on ad spend, and examining product and customer return patterns. The findings are supported with data visualizations and used to develop strategic recommendations for increasing profits, optimizing advertising investments, and reducing costly product returns.
